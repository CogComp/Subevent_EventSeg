<HEADLINE>
Hamas military wing warns to continue rocket attack at Israel
</HEADLINE>
<TEXT>
<P>
GAZA, Nov. 21 (Xinhua)
</P>
<P>
A military wing of the ruling  Palestinian Islamic Resistance Movement
(Hamas) warned on Tuesday  it will continue launching home-made
rockets against Israel.
</P>
<P>
Abu Obaida, a spokesman for Izz el-Deen al-Qassam Brigades,  told a
news conference that his militias will double their attacks against
Israel, especially at Sderot city which is adjacent to  northeast Gaza
Strip.
</P>
<P>
However, Abu Obaida said they can halt rocket attacks if Sderot
residents emigrated from their town.
</P>
<P>
During the conference, the armed wing of the Hamas movement  claimed
responsibility for firing rockets into Sderot Tuesday  morning,
wounding a man seriously.
</P>
<P>
In addition, al-Quds Brigades of the Islamic Jihad (Holy War)
movement also claimed responsibility for firing rockets into  Sderot.
</P>
<P>
Meanwhile, Israeli troops are still operating in northern Gaza  Strip
after entering the area over the past couple of days.
</P>
<P>
Security sources said the Israeli forces rolled into open  fields in
northern Gaza towns of Beit Lahiya and Beit Hanoun.
</P>
<P>
Israeli sources said two soldiers were lightly wounded after
Palestinian militants hurled grenades at a group of soldiers who  took
up position in a makeshift house.
</P>
